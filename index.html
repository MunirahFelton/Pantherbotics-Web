NPHS Robotics is an after-school club that enables students to have a hands on experience with industry level technologies.

***

Our members will learn the complex design process that is vital for the creation of high-level stem projects. Using Computer Aided Design(CAD) we meticulously develop our mechanisms in a way that encourages our members to think through every step of the process.

***

Many of the parts are used in our projects are manufactured within our club. It is our goal to teach our members many of the skills that they will need in a STEM oriented career.
