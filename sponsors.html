California Community College System:
The CCC is a postsecondary education system that includes the Board of Governors of the California Community Colleges and 72 community college districts. The districts have established 114 community colleges throughout California. https://www.cccco.edu/


HAAS Automation Inc:
Gene Haas, the founder of Haas Automtion Inc., created it in 1983 with the purpose of manufacturing economical and reliable machine tool. The Haas Automation produces more than 13,000 machines per year that are engineered for simple maintenance, easy setup, and low-cost operation. https://www.haascnc.com/index.html


Rainbow Transgenic Flies Inc:
The RTF Inc. has been serving the Drosophlia research community since 2004. They have multiple choices of lines for P-element-, PhiC31-, and CRISPR/Cas9-based injections with the best price for fly research community https://www.rainbowgene.com/
